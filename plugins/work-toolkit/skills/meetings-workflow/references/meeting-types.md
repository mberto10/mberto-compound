# Meeting Types Reference

## Regular Meetings

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **JF (Jour Fixe)** | Weekly | 45-60 min | Project sync, blockers, decisions |
| **Daily Standup** | Daily | 15 min | Quick status, blockers |
| **Sprint Planning** | Bi-weekly | 2h | Plan next sprint |
| **Retro** | Bi-weekly | 1h | Process improvement |
| **Lenkungsausschuss** | Monthly | 1-2h | Steering committee, strategy |

## Meeting Preparation Checklist

### Before Meeting
- [ ] Review Linear/YouTrack for updates
- [ ] Prepare agenda (send 24h before)
- [ ] Identify decisions needed
- [ ] Prepare data/metrics
- [ ] Book room/send invite

### During Meeting
- [ ] Start on time
- [ ] Stick to agenda
- [ ] Capture decisions
- [ ] Assign action items
- [ ] End on time

### After Meeting
- [ ] Send follow-up within 24h
- [ ] Update tickets with decisions
- [ ] Create action item tasks
- [ ] Schedule follow-ups if needed

## Agenda Time Allocation

### JF (45 min)
```
Status:      10 min (20%)
Blockers:    10 min (20%)
Decisions:   15 min (35%)
Next Steps:  10 min (20%)
```

### Stakeholder Update (30 min)
```
Summary:     5 min (15%)
Demo:       10 min (35%)
Discussion: 10 min (35%)
Next Steps:  5 min (15%)
```

### Lenkungsausschuss (60 min)
```
Overview:   10 min (15%)
Projects:   30 min (50%)
Decisions:  15 min (25%)
AOB:         5 min (10%)
```
